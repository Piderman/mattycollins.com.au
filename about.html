I'm Matty, a frond-end developer from Melbourne, Australia. My spirit animal is a giraffe (clearly), I have blue hair with a mohawk, ride a hipster banana board and listen to pop-punk or chiptunes.

Preface

I completed a degree in Games and Interactivity (Arts) in 2010, with subjects scattered across the history of media in Australia, 3D modeling and animation, game design, and web development. I have dabbled in web for a long time, starting with table layouts and Frontpage and like most people, I got into web because my band needed a website. But I really took to it after learning about semantics and what you can do with CSS.

Work

I started off as a wee front-end developer at Mintleaf Studio stumbling around a CMS I had never used and editing CSS I barely understood. I quickly weaseled my way up to lead developer on some large projects when we expanded into a new studio Websilk. I now lead mobile development as well as being the go-to guy for accessibility. We use .Net and the beast that is Visual Studio, I don't mean to hate on it but it was not made for CSS.
